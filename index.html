Hmong in Central Valley

Did you know that there are more Hmongs in the Central Valley California than any other states here in the U.S? In Fresno County alone, there are almost 1 million residents here. Of that 1 million, 10% are asians. of that 10% asian population, 40% are Hmongs. 40,000 hmongs in Fresno County alone! Now of course, of that 40,000 hmong residents, not all will speak english. I myself estimated that 30% of the hmong population here does not speak English. That is about 12,000 people!

If you provide a social service or a business catering to the general public, what can you do to tap into the market involving these 12,000 non english speaking hmong?
